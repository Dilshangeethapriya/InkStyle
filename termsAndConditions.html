1. Appointments and Cancellations

All appointments must be made in advance through our booking system. Cancellations or reschedules should be made at least 24 hours before the appointment time. Late cancellations may result in a partial service charge.

2. Deposits and Payments

Some services, such as tattoo sessions, may require a deposit to confirm your booking. Deposits are non-refundable but can be transferred if the appointment is rescheduled within the allowed time frame.
